4 Bedrooms / 3 Baths / 8 Guests

1900 Ninety Eight is newly opened in 2017, the first beachfront development in Destin for almost 10 years. There are only 16 total residences, two for each floor, with every home boasting panoramic views of the Gulf of Mexico. 801 is located on the 8th floor and features 4 bedrooms, 3 bathrooms, and over 2,600 square feet of vacation living space. Modern finishes like marble vanities in the bathrooms, custom kitchen cabinets, and stainless appliances are part of what makes this a truly luxurious vacation experience.

The master bedroom has a king bed, flat screen TV, and a private entrance to expansive balcony. The furnished balcony has an electric grill, two lounge chairs, and an outdoor dining table with six chairs. The master bathroom has double vanities, a soaking tub, and large walk-in shower.

The third bathroom has a shower tub combination and is easy to access from the bedrooms or living areas. The last guest bedroom is furnished with twin bunk beds.
